Welcome!

So what is this website?

This website is my online personal portfolio. I created it as part of an assignment but, it is meant to showcase my skills and abilities. This site is made with CSS, HTML, JavaScript, jQuery, and Bootstrap. I picked out the color scheme and created each page layout. I learned a lot of new skills while creating this website. I hope you enjoy looking through my pictures and looking at my web design work. Check out the About Me page for my contact information.

And what about the featured pictures?

All of the pictures on this website were taken or created by me. The page logo was created in Adobe Illustrator. The pictures in the carousel on this page were both taken and edited by me. The butterfly picture was taken at Disney World. The bench picture was taken at Olivet Nazarene University in Bourbonnais, Illinois. The woods picture was taken in Geneseo, Illinois.
